Arc de Triomf

Constructed for the 1888 Barcelona World Fair by architect Josep Vilaseca i Casanovas, the triumphal arch stands on a wide promenade leading to the Ciutadella Park, where many locals like to spend their weekends with picnic baskets or simply enjoying the beautiful scenery in a green haven in the middle of the buzzing city.
